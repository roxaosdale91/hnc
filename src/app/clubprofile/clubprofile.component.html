Club Profile

The Rotary Club of Quiapo Plaza Miranda was chartered on April 28, 2014. The club was cited Most Outstanding Rookie Club and its Charter President Most Outstanding Rookie President during RY 2014-2015. The Club was also given twenty-five (25) other awards in recognition of its various accomplishments in all avenues of service including the Rotary Foundation. In succeeding Rotary years, the club continued to be cited as one of Most Outstanding Rotary Clubs in District 3810.


The club membership is a mix of men and women who run their respective businesses in various platforms and categories such as Corporate, Government Office, Government Corporation, and the Cabinet and Constitutional bodies.
