VALUES

We are guided by a set of principles and values that unite us for a common purpose. These serve as our direction whenever we take action.


At the Rotary Club of Quiapo Plaza Miranda, we adhere, practice, and observe the Rotary Four Way Test, an ethical guide which we use for our personal and professional relationships.


Of the things we think, say or do:
1. Is it the TRUTH?
2. Is it FAIR to all concerned?
3. Will it build GOODWILL and BETTER FRIENDSHIPS?
4. Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?
