Our services towards our local communities revolve around 7 Areas of Focus:

Basic Education and Literacy

  • Knowledge in a Shoebox
    The club provides educational materials that are placed in a shoebox. This project was launched in year 2016 and has since been adopted by the succeeding club presidents up until the present term, year 2020-2021.

Peace and Conflict Prevention & Resolution

  • Legal Caravan and Lectures, online via Zoom and live streaming on Youtube
  • Interfaith Dialogue, with a follow through session on Magkaibigan Tayo project

Disease Prevention and Treatment

  • Cataract Operations
  • Kidney Health Awareness Lecture, online via Zoom and live streaming on Youtube
  • Construction of a mini clinic within the San Roque Parish compound, plus a Toilet & Wash structure in the same area

Water and Sanitation

  • Construction of restroom and wash areas in San Roque Parish, Blumentritt, Manila

Maternal and Child Health

  • Family Planning Seminar
  • Lecture on Natal and Neo-Natal Care
  • Adopt / sponsor pregnant mothers

Economic and Community Development

  • Coffee selling
  • Pera sa Basura
  • Carpentry

Environment Preservation

  • Tree Planting
