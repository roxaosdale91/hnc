SERVICE ABOVE SELF

We, the members of the Rotary Club of Quiapo Plaza Miranda, believe in pushing ourselves beyond our comfort zones for the betterment of others.

This is compassion. This is selflessness. This is SERVICE ABOVE SELF.

We aim to improve the quality of life among the people in the communities we serve by promoting economic self-sufficiency.

As members of The Rotary Club of Quiapo Plaza Miranda, which is part of a global network, we speak loud and proud through the actions we take.

The Rotary International Theme for RY 2022-2023:

RI President Jennifer Jones imagines a Rotary where members act to make their dreams become reality and they make the most of their club experiences. She urges members to engage more with each other and use these connections to build partnerships that change the world.
